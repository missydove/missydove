About

Hi I am Melissa Melting-Tallow. I am senior at Eastern Washington University. I am pursuing a Bachelor’s in Visual Communication Design. I am also a member of board of directors of my non-profit dance studio, where I created their logo, do their brochures, and update their social media content. I am a risk taker and very optimistic because I have ran my own business in event planning and rented out vendor booths. In the past I have volunteered to help people find work or get training for future employment. Love helping people because I have enjoyed volunteering in the Big Brothers and Big Sisters program, Vanessa Behan Pumpkin Ball, Tom’s Turkey Drive, animal shelters, and pow-wows. I have lots of experience in sales and customer service. I even worked at as a Black Jack dealer for a few years! My goals are to design web sites, business identity, and advertising for non-profits and small businesses. I am proud to be a mother, designer, and artist!

If you like to follow me on facebook my user name is Ms.MeltingTallow.
